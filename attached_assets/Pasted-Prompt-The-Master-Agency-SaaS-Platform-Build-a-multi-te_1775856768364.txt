Prompt: The "Master Agency" SaaS Platform
Build a multi-tenant SaaS platform called [Your Brand Name] (focused on Real Estate AI but expandable to other sectors).

1. Multi-Tenant Architecture & Auth
Stack: React (Frontend), Node.js/Express (Backend), PostgreSQL (Database).

Authentication: Use Clerk to manage "Organizations."

The Principal (Admin) can invite Staff Members (Agents) via email.

Data must be strictly siloed by agency_id.

2. Seat-Based & Usage Billing (Stripe Integration)
Setup Fee: One-time checkout of $1,500 AUD for Training & Onboarding.

Subscription Logic:

Base: $299/mo (Includes 1st User).

Additional Seats: $89/user/month (automatically calculated based on Clerk organization size).

Metered Billing: Track AI minutes. Include 100 mins/mo. Trigger $25 per 10-minute block overage.

Invoicing: Automate GST-compliant Tax Invoices sent via Stripe.

3. AI Receptionist (Voice & Chat)
Integration: OpenAI (LLM) + Vapi.ai (Voice) + Twilio (Phone Numbers).

CRM Sync: Create a bridge for VaultRE API to fetch listings (Address, Price, Inspection Times, Agent Contact).

The "Tenant" Path: - If a user asks to apply, provide the RealEstate.com.au link.

Offer a "Paper/PDF" option. If selected, trigger an email via SendGrid with the NSW Fair Trading Standard Tenancy Form and a custom Agency PDF attached.

Live Handoff: If a "hot lead" is detected (e.g., "I want to make an offer"), the AI must trigger a call transfer to the Listing Agent's mobile.

4. Principal Dashboard (The Command Centre)
Lead Inbox: Show all AI chat/voice transcripts and call recordings.

Form Log: Track which prospective tenants requested paper applications.

Staff Management: Add/Remove agents (this must sync with Stripe billing quantity).

Billing Tab: Show usage credits and history.

5. Legal & Onboarding (Master Website)
Sign-up Funnel: Step-by-step onboarding (Agency Details -> User Count -> Payment).

Legal Gate: A mandatory checkbox for SaaS Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy (Australian Privacy Act & AI Disclosure compliant).

Resources: A tab to download the "Client Manual PDF."

6. Design Aesthetic
Style: "Professional Enterprise."

Colors: Deep Navy, Slate Gray, and Emerald Green (success/growth color).

Responsive: Optimized for Principals viewing leads on their iPhones while on the road.
