## Product 1 — Voice AI Receptionist (Sarah)

**Tag:** Core · Included in all plans
**Pitch:** "Your agency never misses a call again."

Sarah is a natural-sounding AI that answers every inbound call 24/7 — even at 2am on a Sunday. She qualifies the caller (price range, timeline, urgency), answers property questions using live VaultRE data, books inspection times, and emails a full transcript plus contact details the moment the call ends.

**Key capabilities:**
- Answers every call in under 3 seconds
- Qualifies buyer intent (price range, timeline, investment vs owner-occ)
- Books inspections directly from VaultRE calendar
- Emails full transcript + lead profile to agency instantly
- Handles after-hours, overflow, and weekend calls
- Hot-transfers urgent/high-intent callers to listing agent's mobile

**Pitch line:**
> "Every missed call is a missed commission. Sarah costs less than one missed sale per year."

**Key capabilities:**
- Bi-directional sync — updates flow both ways
- Listings, prices, agent contacts always current
- New leads from Sarah appear in VaultRE pipeline
- No manual data entry
- Works with existing VaultRE setup — no migration

**Pitch line:**
> "Other AI tools use static scripts. Sarah reads your actual live data — she knows about the listing that just came on market this morning."
